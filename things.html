BEST TO EXPERIENCE IN MOROCCO

  • Get lost in the Fes Medina – A medieval city full of mystery and charm.
  • Camel trek in the Sahara Desert – Overnight under the stars in Merzouga.
  • Stay in a traditional riad – Cozy, ornate, and full of Moroccan hospitality.
  • Visit Jardin Majorelle in Marrakech – Colorful, serene, and Instagram-worthy.
  • Wander the blue city of Chefchaouen – Dreamy streets and mountain views.
  • Explore the souks of Marrakech – Endless alleys of rugs, spices, and treasures.
  • Watch sunset over the dunes of Erg Chebbi – Pure magic in the desert.
  • Tour the Hassan II Mosque in Casablanca – Oceanfront and jaw-droppingly grand.
  • Hike the High Atlas Mountains – Trek to Berber villages or even Mount Toubkal.
  • Relax in a Moroccan hammam – Deep-clean steam bath and spa treatment.
  • Visit Ait Benhaddou – A stunning ancient ksar and film location.
  • Take a cooking class – Master Moroccan tagine, pastilla, and mint tea.
  • Explore the coastal town of Essaouira – Chill vibes, seafood, and sea breeze.
  • See the Roman ruins of Volubilis – Well-preserved mosaics and columns.
  • Eat street food in Djemaa el-Fnaa – Snails, harira soup, grilled meats.
  • Experience the Sahara by 4×4 – Adventure through sand dunes and oases.
  • Visit the leather tanneries in Fes – Colorful, old-school craftsmanship.
  • Drive the Tizi n'Tichka Pass – Mountain roads with epic views.
  • Tour the Ouzoud Waterfalls – Gorgeous falls surrounded by monkeys and nature.
  • Sip mint tea on a rooftop café – Preferably at sunset, with city views.
